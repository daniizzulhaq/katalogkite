🪁 About Sky Dream

Our journey to bring kite art to Indonesia and the world. From local festivals to international competitions, we continue to innovate and share the beauty of traditional Indonesian kites.

Our Story

Sky Dream or known by the name Rishad, is a kite community and business born out of a deep love for traditional Indonesian art. We don't just sell kites, we also preserve culture and develop innovations in modern kite design.

From small participation in local festivals, we have now grown into a major player in the Indonesian kite industry. We are proud to have represented Indonesia at various international festivals and remain committed to bringing Indonesia's name to the global stage.
